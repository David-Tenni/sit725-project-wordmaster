Game Instructions: Word Master 🎮📚✨

Welcome to Word Master, the ultimate vocabulary challenge! 🧠✨ Compete against others to think of the longest words within specific categories. Each round is fast-paced and thrilling—can you outwit your opponents and claim victory? 🏆🔥

How to Play 🎯
Objective

📝 Score points by submitting the longest words in a quick time for the given category.

🥇 The player with the most points at the end of 5 rounds wins!

Rounds ⏱️

1️⃣ Each game consists of 5 rounds.

2️⃣ Either you can play a random category or select a category of your preference

3️⃣ At the start of each round, a sub category (e.g., “🌎 Countries”) will appear.

4️⃣ You’ll have 3 seconds ⏳ to prepare before the round begins.

Submitting Your Word ✍️

Once the round starts, you’ll have 30 seconds ⏲️ to think of and submit a word.

💡 The longer your word and faster you input, the more points you earn:

Example: “🐈 Cat” = 3 points; “🇦🇺 Australia” = 9 points.

Be quick! ⚡ If another player submits the same word, you’ll need to think of a new one.

Scoring 📊❌

After each round, scores will be displayed as a colorful bar graph 📈:

Your bar grows based on the points scored.

Winning the Game 🏅🌟

The game ends after 5 rounds.

Play until the final round and score as many points as possible! 🕹️✨

Important Rules 📜🔍

1️⃣Words must match the category provided.

2️⃣ Words with symbols or spaces do not require the symbols or spaces to be typed:

 Example: “🇺🇸 United States” → You can type “unitedstates.”

3️⃣ Misspelled words will not be accepted. ❌

Additional Features 🚀
Leaderboard 🏆

Track your performance in real-time! 📊

View rankings by high score, longest word, total wins, and more.

Get notifications 🔔 if your ranking drops!

Multiplayer Simulation 🤝

While playing, opponents’ answers will be simulated using real player data for a seamless experience.

Are you ready to put your vocabulary skills to the test? 🧠🎉

🌟 Let the Word Master challenge begin! 🌟
